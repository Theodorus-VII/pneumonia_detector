AI-Powered Pneumonia Detector

The AI-Powered Pneumonia Detector is a web application that uses artificial intelligence to diagnose pneumonia from chest radiography. It is fast, accurate, and easy to use.

What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is an inflammatory condition of the lung affecting primarily the small air sacs known as alveoli. Symptoms typically include some combination of productive or dry cough, chest pain, fever and difficulty breathing. The severity of the condition is variable. Pneumonia is usually caused by infection with viruses or bacteria and less commonly by other microorganisms, certain medications or conditions such as autoimmune diseases.

For more information, visit: https://www.nhlbi.nih.gov/health/pneumonia

Who we are

This project was developed by a group of 3 Software Engineering students from Addis Ababa University for a course project. We wanted to learn more about machine learning and deep learning, and apply them to a real-world problem.

This website is a dedicated online resource for quick and reliable pneumonia assessments. Our AI-driven tool is designed to assist you in identifying potential signs of pneumonia, allowing for early awareness and prompt medical attention.

Simply upload your chest X-ray or scan using our intuitive interface. Our advanced AI system analyzes the images, detecting patterns associated with pneumonia, and provide an instant and clear assessment of your pneumonia risk, empowering you to make informed decisions about your health. Take the first step towards proactive healthcare with this tool. Early detection matters, and we're here to help.

However, our system is not a replacement for normal medical diagnosis, and it should not be used as the sole basis for any health decisions. We always recommend that users consult a doctor for professional advice and treatment.

What the tool does

Upload your chest X-ray

You can upload your chest X-ray image in JPG, PNG or JPEG format. The image should be clear and show the entire chest area.

Get instant results

Our AI model will analyze your chest X-ray image and give you a probability score for pneumonia. You will also see a heatmap that highlights the affected areas.

Consult a doctor

Based on our testing, our algorithm correctly identifies pneumonia when it exists 95% of the time. If the tool detects that the X-ray has a high probability of pneumonia, you should consult a doctor as soon as possible.
