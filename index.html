The AI-Powered Pneumonia Detector is a web application that uses artificial intelligence to diagnose pneumonia from chest radiography. It is fast, accurate, and easy to use.

What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is an inflammatory condition of the lung affecting primarily the small air sacs known as alveoli. Symptoms typically include some combination of productive or dry cough, chest pain, fever and difficulty breathing. The severity of the condition is variable. Pneumonia is usually caused by infection with viruses or bacteria and less commonly by other microorganisms, certain medications or conditions such as autoimmune diseases.

What the tool does
Upload your chest X-ray

You can upload your chest X-ray image in JPG, PNG or JPEG format. The image should be clear and show the entire chest area.

Get instant results

Our AI model will analyze your chest X-ray image and give you a probability score for pneumonia. You will also see a heatmap that highlights the affected areas.

Consult a doctor

If you have a high probability of pneumonia, you should consult a doctor as soon as possible.
